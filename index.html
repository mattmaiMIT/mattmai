Hello, welcome to my project page! I'm currently a M.S. candidate at MIT Integrated Design & Management, a program fostering product leadership through human-centered design.

Prior to joining MIT, I was the product director at Origin Wireless, Inc., a tech-startup pioneering in non-wearable, camera-free sensing for human motion and vital signs through Wi-Fi. Origin Wireless was one of my focused portfolio companies while I oversaw post-investment acceleration at Foxconn’s strategic investment unit in Cambridge, MA. during 2013 - 2016.

“The Hex Home security is impressed for its Wi-Fi technology, which offers a high level of protection with only a couple of devices!"

Hex Home is a DIY home security system that comprices two devices: the Command and a Sense plug-in sensor. They use Wi-Fi radio waves to monitor a home environment. When someone walks through the Wi-Fi waves, Hex Home analyses the disruptions and immediately informs the user of movement. The app-controlled devices are based on AI technology and have been minimalistically designed in neutral white and with round edges.

Matt Mai | Product Director | Project period: May 2020 - Jul 2021
